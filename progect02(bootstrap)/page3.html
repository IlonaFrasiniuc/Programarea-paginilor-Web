Other help

If you have problems that are difficult for you to cope with or need to speak out, our friendly team is ready to help you.

Free consultations of psychologists in online format or by phone are widespread everywhere, since not everyone has the opportunity to consult a specialist in person.

It is enough to fill out the form and describe your problem on our vk page (https://vk.com/enjoi_life) and we will contact you.

Such a consultation with a psychologist has a number of undeniable advantages:

  • Anonymous communication: if you are embarrassed to talk to a psychologist offline, an online consultation will help start a conversation about an exciting problem without revealing your identity.
  • Free: This is the main advantage of online consultation and needs no explanation.
  • Saving the result: you can only remember the conversation in the psychologist's office, and online communication is available for viewing at any time if you missed something important.
